Tarantulas Spider

Tarantulas Comprise A Group Of Large And Often Hairy Spiders Of The Family Theraphosidae. As Of December 2023, 1,100 Species Have Been Identified, With 166 Genera.

Clade: Avicularioidea

Class: Arachnida
